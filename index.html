Research

Daomorph Research Group develops a formal model of consciousness and explores the limits of rationality.

Its method integrates logical analysis, phenomenology, and structural realism, forming a new epistemic-ontological framework for understanding reality.

The project is conceived as an open space for philosophical interaction, where ideas can evolve and be critically discussed.

Its mission is to contribute to the metarational turn of humanity, moving beyond the constraints of classical reason toward the deeper structures of knowledge and being.
